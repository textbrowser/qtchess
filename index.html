QtChess

QtChess is a peer-to-peer chess program. Written in C++, it sports a 2-D OpenGL/Qt interface and utilizes TCP for communications.

Releases Releases

Welcome to QtChess

QtChess is available on a variety of operating systems, including FreeBSD, MacOS, Linux, and Windows.
